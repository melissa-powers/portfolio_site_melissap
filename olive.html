Olive

A conceptual health and wellness app designed to simplify how users manage their healthcare in a calm, accessible way.

Project Overview

My Role

UI/UX Designer — responsible for user research, wireframing, prototyping, and visual design.

Tools

Figma, Adobe Creative Suite

Timeline

Concept project — Career Foundry UX Design Program

Purpose & Objective

Olive was born from the idea that managing your healthcare shouldn't feel overwhelming. The goal was to create an experience that feels calm, supportive, and intuitive — helping users book appointments, track wellness goals, and access health resources all in one place.

The Challenge

Many existing health apps feel clinical and complex, creating friction for users who are already stressed about their health. Olive needed to feel different — approachable, warm, and easy to navigate, even for users who aren't tech-savvy.

The Solution

By focusing on a clean, organic visual language with soft greens and natural tones, Olive puts users at ease from the first interaction. The interface prioritizes simplicity, with clear pathways for booking appointments and managing care — reducing cognitive load and building trust.
