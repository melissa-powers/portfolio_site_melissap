About Me

My Story

I'm Melissa Powers, a UI/UX Designer focused on crafting calm, inviting experiences that bring comfort and clarity to everyday interactions. I believe that good design should feel effortless — guiding people naturally through each moment without adding stress or confusion.

My journey into design started with a love for problem-solving and empathy for the people using the products I help create. I'm drawn to projects that make a real difference in people's daily lives, especially in health, wellness, and accessibility.
